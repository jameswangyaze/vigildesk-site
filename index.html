Common questions

Will customers know it's an AI?

Yes — every call opens with a clear AI disclosure ("This is an AI-powered assistant for [Business Name]..."). It's required for TCPA compliance and customers actually appreciate the honesty.

Do I have to change my phone number?

No. Calls forward from your existing business line. SMS replies and confirmations also come from your business number — not a random platform shortcode like our competitors.

What if the AI gets confused?

If a caller asks something off-script, the AI either takes a callback message or transfers to your designated emergency line. We monitor every call and you get a daily report of anything flagged.

Are calls recorded?

Yes, with consent disclosed at the start of each call (per California Penal Code 632). Recordings are kept for 90 days for quality and dispute purposes, then auto-deleted. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

How does the 3-job guarantee work?

If VigilDesk doesn't book you at least 3 paying jobs in your first 30 days, your first month is free. Period. No fine print.
